Rei Kawakubo is a Japanese fashion designer based in Tokyo and Paris.

Founder of the brand Comme des Garçons and the concept Dover Street Market.

She founded her first store in Tokyo in 1975, exclusively designing women's clothing.

Three years later, she stormed Paris Fashion Week and opened a boutique. Comme des Garçons became iconic for its anti-fashion and deconstructed, austere pieces.

Revolutionizing fashion with unseen designs and stronger aesthetics.

Rei created Dover Street Market, blending Comme des Garçons' avant-garde design with a department store experience.
